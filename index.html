Our Services

Core Cutting

Precision circular cutting for reinforced concrete, masonry, and asphalt. Our diamond core drilling technology ensures smooth, accurate holes with no vibration.

Key Applications:

  • HVAC ducts, plumbing pipes, and electrical cables.
  • Anchor bolt holes and structural testing samples.
  • Sizes ranging from 10mm to 1000mm diameter.
  • Depth capabilities for thick walls and foundations.

Wall Sawing

Track-mounted cutting systems for precise openings in vertical and steep surfaces. Ideal for heavy reinforced concrete cutting.

Key Applications:

  • Door, window, and ventilation openings.
  • Staircase removals and elevator shaft modifications.
  • Precision cutting up to 700mm depth.
  • Dust-free and vibration-free operation.
Request Quote

Slab & Floor Sawing

High-performance walk-behind saws for cutting horizontal concrete surfaces, roads, and airport runways.

Key Applications:

  • Expansion joints and utility trenches.
  • Demolition of bridge decks and damaged pavement.
  • Cutting reinforced concrete floors for modification.
  • Diesel and electric options for indoor/outdoor use.
Request Quote

Controlled Demolition

Safe and efficient removal of concrete structures using specialized robotic equipment and non-explosive methods.

Key Applications:

  • Partial removal of walls, floors, and beams.
  • Robotic demolition for hazardous or confined areas.
  • Chemical bursting and hydraulic crushing.
  • Complete debris removal and site clearance.
